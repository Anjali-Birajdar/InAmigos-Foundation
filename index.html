About Us

Founded on 23 September 2020 by Mr. Govind Shukla, InAmigos Foundation is a Section 8 registered non-profit organization committed to creating meaningful social impact.

Headquartered in Chhattisgarh and working across India, our mission is to improve lives through education, community development, women empowerment, environmental sustainability, animal welfare, and skill development.

Our Initiatives

Every project reflects our commitment to building stronger communities and creating sustainable change.

Project SEVA

Supporting underprivileged communities through food drives, clothing distribution and community outreach programmes.

Project BACHPANSHALA

Helping children access education, digital literacy and life-skill training for a brighter future.

Project JEEV

Caring for stray animals through feeding, rescue initiatives and volunteer support.

Project UDAAN

Empowering women through self-help groups, skill development and awareness programmes that encourage financial independence.

Project PRAKRITI

Promoting environmental sustainability through plantation drives, awareness campaigns and eco-friendly initiatives.

Project VIKAS

Creating opportunities through internships and skill development programmes that help students prepare for their future careers.
